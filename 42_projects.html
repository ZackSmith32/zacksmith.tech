42 Projects

42 was founded in France 6 years ago as a free coding "University" (though unecredited), where it caries the reputation of a university computer science program. 42 has since set up a campus in Fremont, CA, where it will broaden the reputation of 42.

42 is an opionated school. From my experience the two strongest principles of the school, are learning through understanding, and self reliance. The vast majority of 42 projects are in C, a low level language that forces programmers to understand how the computer works. Additionally 42 has no teachers, just a computer lab, some online lessons, and a group of highly motivated students. There is a rigorous tryout process for 42 which involves a month long sprint (400+ hrs in a month). The students who make it through the "Piscene" (swimming pool) are extremely motivated. We teach each other, compete with each other, and strive to finish projects quickly, and build side projects.
