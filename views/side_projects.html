PostmatesDash

Personal project

I started developing this site in resopnse to my desire to see more detailed information about the deliveries I was doing for Postmates. The site collects users' data through a form, stores the data in a data base and recalls the data into a dashboard. The site allows users to optimize their driving schedule and technique. A side benefit to the site is the aggregation of multiple users' data which gives statistically stronger signals about delivey trends.
