A little about me

Economist turned engineer

For the first five years of my career I built models and analyzed data to support business decisions. It may have been the companies I worked for, but after five years of doing that I had very little satisfaction in my day to day work.

After investigating the careers of my friends, and evaluating my personal strengths, I decided to redirect my career towards software engineering. Over the last 18 months I have been building my technical acumen. In July 2016 I started studying at a revolutionary school called 42 which has forever changed my life (more on that here).
